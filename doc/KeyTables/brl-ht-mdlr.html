Notes

  • The 16-key pad is arranged in four columns and four rows. The keys in the first (top) row are named: B9, One, Two, Three. The keys in the second row are named: B10, Four, Five, Six. The keys in the third row are named: B11, Seven, Eight, Nine. The keys in the fourth (bottom) row are named: B12, B13, Zero, B14.
